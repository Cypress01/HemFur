// how it works

The Process

01

Send Your Ref

Reach out with your character reference sheet and the product tier you want. The more detail, the better.

02

Quote & Deposit

A custom quote is sent based on complexity. A 30–50% non-refundable deposit secures your spot in queue.

03

WIP Updates

Progress photos are sent at key stages — foam sculpt, fur mockup, and final assembly — for your approval.

04

Ship & Wear

Final payment before shipping. Your suit is carefully packaged and sent to your door. Time to suit up.

// terms of service

Good to Know

Payment

  • 30–50% non-refundable deposit required to start
  • Balance due before shipping
  • Payment plans available — ask at inquiry
  • Accepted via PayPal, Venmo, or agreed platform

Turnaround

  • Estimated 3–6 months depending on queue
  • WIP photos shared at every major milestone
  • Rush orders considered case-by-case (extra fee)
  • Queue position updates shared regularly

Your Character

  • Must provide a clear reference sheet before start
  • Minor design tweaks accepted up to sculpt stage
  • Major changes after foam lock may incur extra cost
  • You own your character — we just bring it to life

Shipping & Returns

  • Ships worldwide — buyer covers shipping cost
  • Fully insured and tracked shipping only
  • No refunds once suit is complete and shipped
  • Warranty repairs considered within 30 days
